Who we are

OpenStreetMap Ghana is a not-for-profit working across Ghana promoting community mapping, creating awareness and empowering map reading and map making. We generate Open Data and building a network of mappers in Ghana.

All our activities are set up around the global OpenStreetMap (OSM) project and community. OSM is a map and database whereby the stored geographic data is owned by the contributors themselves: allowing everyone to view, create, use this information for free, in new and innovative ways.

Open Data & FOSS

We are supporters and users of Free and Open Source Software. In fact OpenStreetMap community globally is built upon the principles of Open Source

Community

We are a community of ethusiasts and professionals passionate about Open and Accessible Geo data. We build maps and mappers through community engagement and capacity building.
